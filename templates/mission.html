Our Mission

Empowering Muslims to fulfil their Zakat obligations with clarity, security and trust.

ZakaatIQ was created to help Muslims accurately calculate and manage their Zakat obligations using modern financial technology.

Many Muslims struggle to determine whether their wealth meets the Nisab threshold or how much Zakat they should pay. ZakaatIQ simplifies this process through a secure and transparent platform.

Our system combines financial tools, forecasting models, and verified charity recommendations to support Muslims in fulfilling one of the core pillars of Islam.

By integrating ethical financial guidance with secure technology, ZakaatIQ aims to make Zakat calculation more accessible and trustworthy for the global Muslim community.
