What is Zakat?

Zakat is one of the five pillars of Islam and is a mandatory charitable contribution for eligible Muslims. It requires giving 2.5% of one's qualifying wealth annually to those in need.

It purifies wealth, supports the less fortunate, and strengthens community bonds.

What is Nisab?

Nisab is the minimum amount of wealth a Muslim must possess before they are required to pay Zakat. It is calculated based on the value of gold or silver.

If your total wealth exceeds this threshold for a full lunar year, Zakat becomes obligatory.
